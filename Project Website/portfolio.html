PORTFOLIO

Nepali Paisa application (app) and website provide an innovative online platform, offering easy access to Nepal's stock market news, data, tools, indicators, reports, and more. With an intuitive and user-friendly interface, users can stay up-to-date with the latest trends and insights, ensuring a comprehensive experience of the Nepalese stock market.

Founded in 2011 by Abhijit Shrestha and Ajay Shrestha, Source Code is a pioneering fintech company that develops innovative solutions for capital markets, including tools for stock brokers, merchant bankers, and investors, and focuses on creating a comprehensive data bank of NEPSE-listed companies and executing complex financial and real estate analysis.

Since 1995, TIME Pharmaceuticals has led Nepal's pharmaceutical industry as a WHO-GMP certified entity, creating over 200 products to bolster healthcare and foster national self-sufficiency in vital medicines. With a steadfast dedication to innovation and excellence, they prioritize improving patient health by addressing unmet medical needs.

Attic was established in 2009 in Uttar Dhoka, now located in Gyaneshwor. Attic has evolved from a cozy gathering place to a premier destination for outstanding dining and hospitality. Renowned for their great food, exquisite drinks, and exemplary atmosphere, Attic is the perfect setting for a memorable experience.

Kaffeine was founded in 2013, and has become a cornerstone in the coffee industry. Starting as a small coffee outlet, kaffeine quickly grew, embracing role as educators and suppliers within the coffee community.

Next Holdings Real Estate is a subsidiary of Next Holdings Limited. It is developing Holiday Homes project in Nagarkot.

Kathmandu Lanco Pvt. Ltd is a premier real estate development company involved in the organized housing, apartment, and commercial real estate sectors. In addition to residential housing projects, Kathmandu Lanco’s plans include high quality, aspirational, and innovative mixed use spaces in and beyond the Kathmandu Valley.

Bottlers Nepal (Terai) Limited (BNTL), subsidiary of Bottlers Nepal Limited, is located in Bharatpur, Chitwan. During the time of inception, BNTL started manufacturing its products in RGB (Returnable glass bottles) and eventually in 2013, added a PET packaging line.

Nepal Warehousing Company Limited was established in 2017 with the objective of providing commodity storage services, auction management services, laboratory test services, collateral management services for warehouse receipt financing and commodities fleet management services. The company is developing Silo and related infrastructure and facilities in three locations - 15000 MT capacity each in Sunsari and Kapilvastu and 25000 MT capacity in Parsa.

Shreenagar Agritech Industries Ltd. (SAIL) is Nepal’s leading Agri business company providing integrated services to the farmers. The company aims to be the One Stop Solution for Farmers - providing seed, feed, services and market connections for their growth and success.

Peoples Energy Limited is developing 48.8 MW Khimti-II Hydroelectric Project. The project is located in the border of Ramechhap and Dolakha districts. Khimti River is a tributary of Tamakoshi - a major branch of Saptakoshi river system of Nepal.

Lord Buddha Reinsurance Broking Company received reinsurance broker license from Nepal Insurance Authority in 2022. The company acts as an intermediary between the Insurance Company and the Reinsurance Company.

Annapurna Cable Car was established in 2073 BS to run a cable car from Sedibagar to Sarangkot. It has 17 gondolas that can convey 500 people per hour.
